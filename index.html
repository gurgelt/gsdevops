It's not just about hunger,
It's about humanity.

Hunger is a global problem that affects millions of people around the world. According to the Organization of United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), about 828 million people were affected by hunger in 2021, while 2.3 billion people suffer from moderate or severe food insecurity in 2021.

We are always trying to make hunger less visible in our world!
